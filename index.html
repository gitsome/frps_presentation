Why FPS?

Managing FPS can improve User Experience

  • Flicker detection is generally between 50-60 Hz
  • Below 15 FPS often interpreted as skipping
  • Reduce visual stress / processing load

The Higher the Better

  • 200 Hz seems to be the human limit
  • Diminishing returns at about 60 Hz
  • 100 Hz balancing perception / cost
  • Apple has "ProMotion" devices @ 120 Hz

It's More Than FPS

Continuity of FPS

  • Discontinuity = JANK
  • JANK = Broken
  • Broken = Reduction in Trust
